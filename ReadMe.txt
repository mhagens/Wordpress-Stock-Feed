Show stock market price updates for the DOW on your website

== Description ==

Display stock market data on your Wordpress site. The Dow is probably the most popular benchmark for tracking overall US stock market activity & exchange health. Place the short code [sm_update] to display our widget anywhere on your site.

* Stock information automatically updated every 15 minutes during trading hours
* To add the plugin directly into posts you can also use our Javascript code at isthedow.com 

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/stock-market-updates-dow-jones` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
1. Place [sm_update] anywhere you would like the Stock Market price information to appear.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Widget looks cut off =

Try placing the widget in another location. Your parent divs of your template might be cutting it off.

= How do I use this? =

Activate the plugin and place the shortcode [sm_update] where you would like the stock widget to appear.

= Are quotes realtime =

Quotes are delayed at least 15 minutes from our data provider.
